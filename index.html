Are you a journalsim pre-major taking Journalism 108? This guide will help you get through the chaos of learning the Adobe suite and become a confident #RSJ108 master.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop (preferrably Mac but the RSJ building has them if you don't)
  • Computer mouse (trust me, it makes life 1,000x easier)
  • Adobe suite (yes the entire suite, you'll use it later)
  • A planner or planner software (I recommend Outlook)
  • Twitter app for computers
  • Positive attitue :)

  1. Download all of the following:

    -Adobe Creative Suite, it is $19.99 a month and you don't need a book for the course so it breaks even

    -The syllabus, find it in the modules section of the course on Canvas

    -Outlook (if you choose to use it) and Twitter

  2. Add all due dates in your planner of choice

    Use this video to help organize lectures, challenges, quizzes, and discussions.

  3. Create a Twitter account and fully complete profile setup

    You will use this account to post weekly tweets and interact with other #RSJ108 students. Alternatively, you can use your personal Twitter account, but it's better to start fresh. Think of this as a professional account.

    >

  4. Familiarize yourself with Adobe Creative suite.

    Follow tutorials on this site and even try creating your first design!

    Click here for a tutorial

  5. Be confident in your work!

    As the semester progresses, you'll get better and learn more. As long as you plan ahead, participate, and keep a poitive mindset you can totally rock #RSJ108!
